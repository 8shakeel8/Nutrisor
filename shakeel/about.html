What We Do.

At Nutrisor, we are dedicated to revolutionizing agriculture through advanced technology and data-driven insights. Our website combines cutting-edge sensors and sophisticated data visualization tools to empower farmers and agricultural enthusiasts with real-time information about their crops. Here's how our platform works:

NPK Sensor::

Our NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium) sensor plays a crucial role in understanding the nutritional health of your crops. It continuously monitors the soil's nutrient levels, helping you make informed decisions about fertilizer application and optimizing plant growth.

Moisture Sensor:

Maintaining the right soil moisture level is essential for healthy plant growth. Our moisture sensor provides accurate readings of soil moisture content, allowing you to efficiently manage irrigation schedules and prevent overwatering or underwatering.

pH Sensor:

Soil pH directly influences nutrient availability to plants. Our pH sensor measures the acidity or alkalinity of the soil, enabling you to adjust pH levels to create an optimal environment for plant roots to absorb essential nutrients.

Humidity Sensor:

Humidity levels in the air and soil play a role in determining plant health and disease susceptibility. Our humidity sensor provides insights into the moisture content in the air and soil, helping you prevent issues like mold growth and disease outbreaks.
